Zohran Mamdani: A New York Turning Point

By Jules Abry · Editorial · 7th november 2025

Zohran Mamdani’s election as New York’s first Democrat mayor sparks both hope and controversy.

Zohran Mamdani became the first Democrat Mayor of New York, a decision that sparked controversy among Republicans, who consider him a radical democrat. In my opinion, this is good news, because the city needs a major change. Mamdani comes from a “normal” background: he is not rich, he lives in a small apartment in Queens, and he stays close to the younger population of New York. He also uses social media actively for his campaign.

For the first time during Trump’s mandate, Mamdani has managed to create a strong political opposition against him. He has many great ideas, but if he cannot apply them, it could become a disaster, because many people see hope in him. For now, he continues to perform well in the statistics, so we will see what happens on January 1st, 2026.
