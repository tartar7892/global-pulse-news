AI use is bad and I'm going to tell you why!

By Florian Lucain--Georget · Editorial News · 20th November 2025

An opinion on how current uses of AI risk damaging creativity and human work.

AI can be useful sometimes, but how people currently use it is very dangerous for creativity.

Today, a lot of AI is used for image generation and it’s not a good thing, because I really think AI cannot reproduce human emotion and all the other aspects that make human art. This is exactly the same for AI music; it simply doesn't have human taste.

I also need to talk about the fact that AI is used to replace a job that was done previously by a human. It’s sad because AI is not as good as a human, and it’s terrible for people. Maybe AI can be good for mechanical and repetitive jobs. It will be a good thing if AI replaces that type of job so humans can do more interesting and creative ones, but it seems that the exact opposite is happening right now.

So we really need to change our use of AI, because it will destroy creativity while in fact it could help us do better things.
